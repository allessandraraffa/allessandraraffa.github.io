For the academic discipline, see Church history. For other uses, see History of Christianity. Christian History is a magazine on the history of Christianity. It was established by Ken Curtis in 1982 and published by the Christian History Institute. From 1989 to 2008 it was published by Christianity Today International, before being revived once more by the Christian History Institute in 2011.[1] References[edit] Jump up ^ About Us, Christian History Institute.
